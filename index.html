About Anandam Residency

As the best township in Asansol, we are proud to offer a variety of modern home designs for Simplex and Duplex Bungalows that cater to the diverse needs of our residents. From open layouts with plenty of room to entertain, to our Active Adult style homes with all modern facilities, we ensure that every resident finds a home that suits their lifestyle. In addition to our exceptional residential options, we also offer a range of amenities, including a well-equipped fitness center, landscaped gardens, and a community clubhouse, all designed to provide residents with a comfortable and luxurious lifestyle. Furthermore, we understand the importance of accessibility and affordability, which is why we offer affordable low price plots, ensuring that everyone can experience the exceptional living experience at Anandam Residency.
